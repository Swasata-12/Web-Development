Nikola Tesla

1856 - 1943

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and physicist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electrical system.

Key Achievements

  • Invented the Tesla Coil
  • Pioneered AC power systems
  • Developed wireless communication concepts
  • Held over 300 patents worldwide
